Vision

"An Institutional Law Firm that has a Continuing Drive for Justice, Fairness, Professionalism, Integrity, Competency, Promptness and Reliable Legal Service."




Mission

"To be the preferred Law Firm of lawyers, clients and employees, providing quality, reliable, effective and professional legal services, and be recognized as a strong legal institution in the country."


Who we are

NMGRA is a firm composed of lawyers dedicated to the practice of law in the utmost professional manner, with the highest standards of integrity, professionalism and quality service to its clients. The Firm was founded by five partners who have been together in the practice of law for more than a decade. From the Firm's establishment in mid-2005, NMGRA has grown to a complement of eight partners and thirty legal and non-legal support staff. The Firm's partners and associates are graduates of the top law schools in the Philippines, many of whom have post graduate degrees locally, as well as from prestigious schools in Europe and the United States.

What we Do

NMGRA is a full-service law firm, offering a complete range of legal services such as: general counseling; commercial transactions and special projects; litigation and dispute resolution (including arbitration); employment, labor dispute resolution; immigration; corporate secretary and directorship services; intellectual property; as well as taxation and estate planning.
